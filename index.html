Why We Exist

(IF We Exist)

Because writing right now is all to often leading us deeper into the Torment Nexus, and we feel it is best NOT to build the Torment Nexus, we decided to write some books about how to sidestep the Torment Nexus and just go about our business. We are TAMSters and geeks, 90s kids that grew up and realized we'd forgotten why we wanted to grow up. We figured someone better write the stuff that meant so much to us as kids because our kids can't relate to what our parents' generation wrote for us. So the cycle continues.
